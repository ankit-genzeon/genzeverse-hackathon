Sarah the HR Administrator

Background:

Tania is a 35-year-old HR administrator in a medium-sized company. She is responsible for managing employee records, payroll, and ensuring accurate timesheet data for billing and reporting purposes.

Goals:

  • Streamline the timesheet management process.
  • Reduce errors.
  • Generate accurate reports efficiently.

Challenges:

  • Often encounters incomplete or incorrect timesheet submissions.
  • Spends a significant amount of time manually reviewing and correcting timesheet data.

Preferences:

  • Appreciates a chatbot that provides clear instructions to employees.
  • Values features such as automated validation checks and notifications for incomplete or inaccurate entries.
  • A dashboard or reporting functionality would be helpful for generating timesheet reports.
